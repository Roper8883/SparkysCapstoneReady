Premium support note

Current premium offer is a 10-week non-renewing pass. If access appears incorrect, use Restore Purchases first, then contact support.

Premium vs RTO subscription

Learner premium and any future RTO portal subscription are separate products with separate billing and access rights.

Profiling data access restrictions

Weekly profile PDFs are dispatched only to the signed-in learner and linked employer recipients.

SRTO/RTO learner-data access is separate and requires active paid RTO subscription, explicit learner consent, backend authorization, and prior written permission from Spark's Capstone Ready.

Unauthorised access or use of learner profile data is prohibited.
