  • Sign-in/session identifiers and account state.

  • Deliver app features and maintain account security.
  • Operate premium access windows (current offer is non-renewing 10-week pass).
  • Generate/dispatch profile documents to learner + linked employer workflow recipients.
  • Maintain delivery reliability, retry handling, and audit trails.

3. Storage and security

Data is stored on-device first and may sync via Apple iCloud where enabled/available. Sensitive actions can require explicit confirmation and biometric verification where supported.

4. Profile data restrictions

Profile document delivery is restricted to signed-in learner and linked employer recipient workflows. SRTO/RTO use/storage/ingestion requires prior written permission from Spark's Capstone Ready and an active commercial agreement.

5. Account deletion

In-app deletion path: Profile icon -> Account & Data -> Delete Account & Data.
