Privacy Policy

Version 1.4 | Effective date: 26 February 2026

Spark's Capstone Ready (Australia) provides CapstoneReady. Contact: capstoneready@outlook.com.

Instagram: @sparkyscapstoneready | YouTube: Channel | Facebook: Profile

Compliance notice: CapstoneReady is a preparation/training support app and is not a Registered Training Organisation (RTO).

1. Scope

This policy explains how personal information is handled in CapstoneReady in an Australian context, aligned with the Australian Privacy Principles.

2. Information we collect

  • Sign-in identifiers and account profile details from selected providers (Apple, Google, Facebook, Email).
  • Learning progress data including attempts, scores, readiness, and module completion.
  • Weekly profiling records, including recipient emails and supervisor sign-off fields.
  • Notification center and messaging metadata used for inbox sync, signature workflow, and tutorial reminders.
  • Premium entitlement state from Apple StoreKit.
  • Tutorial booking data (for example topic, tutor details, session time, and status events).
  • Operational diagnostics required for support and app stability.

3. Why we use information

  • Provide app access and features.
  • Track progress and training preparation outcomes.
  • Deliver profile export/distribution to the signed-in learner and linked employer.
  • Support separate SRTO/RTO portal workflows only where subscription, learner consent, and backend authorization are valid.
  • Activate and validate purchased premium access (currently a non-renewing 10-week pass).
  • Support notifications and workflow events across profiling, messaging, and tutorial booking.
  • Maintain security controls and service quality.

4. Storage and security

  • Progress/profile records are stored locally on device.
  • Sensitive local payloads are encrypted at rest with Keychain-backed key handling where available.
  • Periodic device presence verification checks are enforced during signed-in sessions (biometric where available).
  • We apply reasonable safeguards against unauthorised access, loss, and misuse.

5. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal information. Data may be disclosed only where required for app operation, configured delivery services, or legal obligations.

6. Profile data access restrictions

  • Direct profile PDF delivery is restricted to signed-in learner and linked employer recipients.
  • SRTO/RTO access is restricted to subscribed, consented, and authenticated portal workflows.
  • Any SRTO/RTO use or storage without prior written permission from Spark's Capstone Ready and an active paid RTO subscription is prohibited.

7. Third-party links

The app may open external services such as YouTube links for tutorial content. Third-party platforms operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

8. Premium and RTO subscription separation

The learner premium pass and any future RTO portal subscription are separate products. A learner premium purchase does not grant RTO portal access, and an RTO portal subscription does not grant learner premium entitlements.

9. Access, correction, complaints

For access/correction/complaints: capstoneready@outlook.com.

10. Updates

We may update this policy. The current version and effective date are shown on this page.
